**How Auto-Renewal Works:**
1. Subscription purchased for initial term (1 month)
2. Benefits active immediately
3. 24 hours before expiration, subscription automatically renews
4. Your Apple Account charged for renewal
5. New subscription period begins immediately
6. Cycle repeats until cancelled

**Important Auto-Renewal Information:**
- Subscriptions renew automatically unless turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
- Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
- Renewal charged at the current subscription price (which may change with notice for new subscribers)
- Your subscription price will not change during your current subscription term
- No refunds for the current subscription period when cancelling

### 6. Refund Policy

All sales are final. However, you may be eligible for a refund in certain circumstances under Apple's refund policy or applicable consumer protection laws.

**Apple's Refund Process:**

Refund requests must be made directly to Apple, not to AiXue AI. We do not have the ability to process refunds.

**To Request a Refund from Apple:**

1. Go to https://reportaproblem.apple.com/
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find the EarthLord purchase you wish to refund
4. Click "Report a Problem"
5. Select the issue type and explain your reason
6. Submit your request
7. Apple will review and respond typically within 48 hours

**Alternative Method:**
- Contact Apple Support directly at https://support.apple.com/

**Refund Eligibility:**

Apple may approve refunds for:
- Accidental purchases (especially by children)
- Technical issues preventing use of purchased content
- Unauthorized purchases
- Duplicate charges
- Billing errors

Apple generally does NOT refund:
- Change of mind after consumption of items
- Items already used or consumed in the game
- Subscriptions where benefits were already received
- Purchases made more than 90 days ago

#### 6.2 Subscription Refunds

**Active Subscriptions:**
- If you cancel a subscription, no refund for the current period

**Trial Period Refunds:**

**California and U.S. Consumer Rights:**
