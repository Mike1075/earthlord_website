### 1. Introduction

Welcome to EarthLord, a location-based outdoor strategy game developed by AiXue AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates.

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Personal Information

**Account Information:**
- Email address (when using email registration)
- Display name (username)
- Profile picture (optional)
- Third-party account information (Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In)

**Location Data:**
- Real-time GPS location (required for core gameplay)
- Location history (to track game progress and territory control)
- Approximate location for regional matchmaking

**Game Data:**
- Player statistics and achievements
- In-game purchases and transaction history
- Resource inventory and territory ownership
- Social interactions (alliances, messages, trades)
- Game progression and level data

**Device Information:**
- Device model and operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information
- App version and crash logs

**Photos and Camera:**
- Photos you choose to upload for territory verification
- Camera access for AR features (optional)

**Communication Data:**
- Push notification tokens
- In-game chat messages
- Customer support communications

#### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use EarthLord:
- IP address
- App usage patterns and session duration
- Performance metrics and diagnostics
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) if you consent

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

**Core Game Functionality:**
- Provide location-based gameplay features
- Match players in your region
- Verify territory control and ownership
- Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
- Save and sync your game progress

**Communication:**
- Send push notifications about game events
- Respond to customer support inquiries
- Send important service announcements
- Notify you about subscription renewals

**Improvement and Analytics:**
- Analyze usage patterns to improve gameplay
- Identify and fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features based on user behavior
- Conduct A/B testing for game balance

**Safety and Security:**
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect against unauthorized access
- Verify account authenticity

**Legal Compliance:**
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Respond to legal requests and prevent harm
- Fulfill tax and accounting obligations

**Backend Services:**

### 5. Data Storage and Security

**Storage Location:**
- Primary data storage: Supabase cloud infrastructure (US/EU)
- Local device cache for gameplay optimization
- Encrypted backups on Apple iCloud (if enabled)

**Security Measures:**
- Industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data transmission
- Encrypted database storage
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure API communications

**Data Retention:**
- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Inactive accounts: Data retained for 2 years after last login, then deleted
- Deleted accounts: Data permanently removed within 30 days
- Transaction records: Retained for 7 years for legal compliance
- Aggregated anonymous analytics: Retained indefinitely

### 7. Children's Privacy

EarthLord is rated 13+ and is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately.

Parents or guardians who believe we may have collected information from a child under 13 should contact us at onestnet@gmail.com.

In the EU, special protections apply to children under 16, and we comply with GDPR requirements for child data processing.

### 8. International Data Transfers

If you access EarthLord from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

For EU users, we ensure adequate protection through:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
- Compliance with GDPR requirements for international transfers
- Data processing agreements with all third-party processors

### 9. Location Data Specific Provisions

**Why We Need Location:**
- Core gameplay requires real-time GPS to interact with territories
- Matching players in your geographic region
- Preventing GPS spoofing and cheating

**Location Permissions:**
- "Always" location access required for background territory monitoring
- Location collected only when app is active or in background (with your permission)

**Controlling Location Access:**
- iOS Settings > EarthLord > Location > Choose permission level
- Disabling location access will prevent core gameplay features

**Location Accuracy:**
- We collect precise location (GPS coordinates)
- Approximate location shown to other players (for privacy)
- Exact location never shared with other players without consent

### 10. Do Not Track Signals

Our application does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals. We track user activity as described in this Privacy Policy to provide game services.
