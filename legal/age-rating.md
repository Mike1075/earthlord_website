## English Version

### 1. Recommended Age Rating

**EarthLord is rated: 13+**

This rating is based on content analysis according to Apple's updated 2025 App Store age rating system and international content rating standards.

### 2. Rating Justification

#### 2.1 Why 13+ Rating?

EarthLord has been classified as suitable for ages 13 and above due to the following content characteristics:

**Themes:**
- Post-apocalyptic setting with mild dystopian themes
- Territory conquest and strategic competition
- Resource management and survival mechanics
- Social interaction and player alliances

**Content Considerations:**
- **Infrequent/Mild Realistic Violence:** Strategic combat is represented abstractly without graphic depiction
- **Infrequent/Mild Fantasy Violence:** Battles between territories/factions shown through animations and statistics
- **Competitive Gameplay:** Players compete for resources and territory control
- **Social Features:** Chat and messaging between players (moderated)
- **In-App Purchases:** Subscriptions and consumable items available

**Why Not Lower (4+ or 9+):**
- Strategic warfare themes, even when abstract, are more appropriate for teens
- Real-world location-based gameplay requires maturity and safety awareness
- Competitive social interaction requires understanding of online etiquette
- In-app purchase mechanics require financial literacy

**Why Not Higher (16+ or 18+):**
- No graphic violence, gore, or realistic weapons
- No sexual or suggestive content
- No profanity or mature language in official content
- No alcohol, tobacco, or drug references
- No horror or intense fear elements
- No simulated gambling with real money

### 6. Parental Guidance and Recommendations

#### For Parents/Guardians

**Before Allowing Your Child to Play:**
1. Review this Age Rating Guide and our Privacy Policy
2. Discuss online safety and appropriate behavior
3. Set up iOS Screen Time parental controls
4. Disable in-app purchases if desired
5. Monitor location permissions and usage
6. Review chat and social features together

**Safety Tips:**
- Accompany children under 16 during outdoor gameplay
- Ensure they don't trespass or enter dangerous areas
- Teach them not to share personal information with strangers
- Set time limits for gameplay
- Monitor in-app purchase activity

**Parental Controls Available:**
- iOS Screen Time: Limit app usage and purchases
- Disable in-app purchases: Settings > Screen Time > Content & Privacy Restrictions
- Disable location services: Settings > Privacy > Location Services > EarthLord
- Restrict chat: Contact support for chat restrictions

**Red Flags to Watch For:**
- Excessive spending on in-app purchases
- Secretive behavior about gameplay
- Neglecting responsibilities or sleep
- Meeting strangers from the game in person
- Sharing personal information

#### Age-Specific Recommendations

**Ages 13-15:**
- Parental supervision recommended
- Review purchases and location activity regularly
- Accompany during outdoor play when possible
- Monitor social interactions in-game

**Ages 16-17:**
- Discuss responsible spending on in-app purchases
- Ensure understanding of subscription auto-renewal
- Safety awareness for outdoor gameplay
- Encourage reporting inappropriate behavior

**Ages 18+:**
- Full independence with recommended safety awareness
- Responsible management of subscriptions and purchases

《地球新主》被归类为适合13岁及以上年龄段，原因如下内容特征：

**主题：**

- 基于现实世界位置的游戏需要成熟度和安全意识

- 应用内购买机制需要财务素养

**为什么不更高（16+或18+）：**
- 无图形暴力、血腥或真实武器
- 无性或暗示性内容
- 官方内容中无亵渎或成人语言
- 无酒精、烟草或毒品参考
- 无恐怖或强烈恐惧元素
- 无真实货币模拟赌博
